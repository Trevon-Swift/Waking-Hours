# Waking Hours · 16‑hour countdown clock

> *“The lesson of time is time only lessens.”*

**Waking Hours** is a philosophy-inspired time tool. Instead of counting up from midnight or down to midnight, it counts down the **16 waking hours** you actually have each day – from the moment you wake up until you sleep. (based on 8-hour sleep routine)


It came from a verse I was writing, one last line (quoted above) that sparked the question: *What if we treated our 16 hours waking hours as the real day?*

---

## 🧠 The idea

Most clocks show 24h (too abstract) or 12h (too familiar). But we’re awake ~16h. That’s the real container for work, creation, movement, reflection – and rest.

This app gives you:

- **A visual arc** – the day draining away, grain by grain  
- **A live countdown** (hours : minutes : seconds left)  
- **An hour‑block allocator** – decide *before* the day starts where your hours go  

It’s part timer, part journal, part ritual.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Set your wake‑up time** – the clock adjusts instantly  
- **Live arc & progress bar** – see your day shrink  
- **16 block allocator** – assign hours to:  
  - Work  
  - Creative  
  - Review  
  - Fitness  
- **Undo** – tap the *Available* card to take back the last hour  
- **Reset all** – start a fresh allocation  
- **Persistent storage** – your allocation and wake time survive a page refresh  
- **Fully responsive** – works on desktop and mobile  

---

## 🎯 Why 16 hours?

Because the other 8 are for sleep, ideally.  
When you frame a day as 16 hours instead of 24, every hour feels more precious. You stop asking *“what did I do all day?”* and start asking *“where did my waking hours go?”*

The quote – *“the lesson of time is time only lessens”* – is the line that sparked this project. It’s not meant to be nihilistic. It’s a call to spend our time deliberately with discernment, instead of letting it waste away with regret.

## 🧭 How to use

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Set wake time | Click *“woke at ––:–– — adjust”*, pick a time, hit Set |
| Allocate an hour | Tap any category card (Work, Creative, Review, Fitness) |
| Undo last move | Tap the **Available** card (shows which category will be undone) |
| Reset all allocations | Click *“reset all”* at the bottom |
| Everything saves automatically | Close and reopen – your choices are still there |

The arc fills as the day passes. When 16 hours are up, the clock goes dark – time to rest.
